FAQ

Frequently asked.

Do I need experience? +

No. The system is designed for complete beginners. If you can press print, you can follow the PolishMaster workflow.

Does this work on PLA? +

Yes. All workflows are optimized for PLA, PETG, and ABS — the three most common materials used by makers worldwide.

Can I sell what I make? +

Yes. Commercial use rights are included in your membership. Sell on Etsy, your own store, or wherever you want.

What tools do I need? +

Basic sanding papers, a polishing kit, and your printer. A full recommended tools list is provided inside the system.
